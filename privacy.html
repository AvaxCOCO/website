Privacy Policy

How we handle your data on the $COCO Leaderboard

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the $COCO Leaderboard ("Leaderboard"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

2. Information We Collect

When you connect your X (formerly Twitter) account to the Leaderboard, we collect:

  • Your X profile information (username, display name, profile picture)
  • Public tweets that mention $COCO or use related hashtags
  • Engagement metrics related to $COCO content (mentions, hashtags, reposts, likes)
  • Leaderboard activity (points earned, ranking)

We do not collect private messages, email addresses, or other non-public information from your X account.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Create and manage your Leaderboard profile
  • Calculate your points based on $COCO engagement
  • Determine your ranking on the Leaderboard
  • Calculate your AVAX allocation bonus for the $COCO presale
  • Improve the Leaderboard functionality and user experience

We do not use your information for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

4. Data Storage and Security

We store your data securely using industry-standard encryption and security measures. Your X authentication tokens are stored securely in your browser's local storage and are not accessible to other websites or applications.

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the Leaderboard service. If you disconnect your X account, your profile data will be removed from our active systems.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With service providers who help us operate the Leaderboard
  • If required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property

Your public Leaderboard ranking and points are visible to other users of the Leaderboard.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Disconnect your X account at any time
  • Object to our processing of your data

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@avaxcoco.com.

7. X API Usage

Our use of X data complies with X's Developer Agreement and Policy. We only access public data with your explicit permission, and we respect X's rate limits and usage guidelines.

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use local storage to maintain your authentication state and Leaderboard preferences. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics services that track your browsing activity across other websites.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at privacy@avaxcoco.com.
