How to Buy $COCO

Follow these simple steps to join the $COCO community on the Avalanche blockchain

1

Buy AVAX

Purchase AVAX on Crypto.com or your preferred centralized exchange. Make sure to buy enough to cover your desired $COCO purchase plus transaction fees.

  • Create an account on Crypto.com
  • Complete verification
  • Buy AVAX with credit card or bank transfer
Visit Crypto.com
2

Set Up Wallet

Download and set up Core Wallet to store your AVAX and $COCO. Core Wallet is the recommended wallet for the Avalanche ecosystem.

  • Download Core Wallet app or extension
  • Create a new wallet and save your seed phrase
  • Add Avalanche C-Chain network
Get Core Wallet
3

Transfer AVAX

Send your AVAX from the exchange to your Core Wallet. Make sure to use the C-Chain address from your Core Wallet.

  • Copy your C-Chain address from Core Wallet
  • Withdraw AVAX from Crypto.com to this address
  • Wait for confirmation (usually takes 1-2 minutes)
4

Swap for $COCO

Use Pharaoh Exchange to swap your AVAX for $COCO tokens. Pharaoh is the exclusive DEX partner for $COCO.

  • Connect your Core Wallet to Pharaoh Exchange
  • Select AVAX as the "From" token and $COCO as the "To" token
  • Enter the amount and confirm the swap
  • Consider providing liquidity to earn additional rewards
Swap on Pharaoh
